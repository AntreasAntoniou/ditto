# Cliphoard — 10 Songs

Ten little anthems for a clipboard manager that hoards everything you copy and
hands it back the instant you need it. Press **⌃⌥⌘V**.

---

## 1. "Hoard It Back" — *power-pop anthem (the title track)*
> **Verse**
> Copied a password, copied a line,
> copied that link at half past nine —
> lost it to the void like every time before…
> not anymore.
>
> **Chorus**
> Cliphoard it — hoard it — never let it go,
> everything you copy, the whole damn show.
> Hit control-option-command-V,
> and the thing you lost comes back to me. 🎶

## 2. "Command Yourself (⌘V)" — *hip-hop*
> Yo — every Ctrl-C is a deposit in the bank,
> on-device, no cloud, you got nobody to thank
> but a little cyan V on a graphite tile,
> two-thousand clips deep and I'm searchin' by *style* —
> not the words, the *meaning*, that's the magpie move,
> reach into the hoard and I got nothing to prove. ⌘V.

## 3. "The Packrat's Lament" — *country ballad*
> I never throw a clipboard away,
> every link and line, I let 'em stay.
> Some call me a hoarder, well that may be true —
> but darlin', when you need it, I'll find it for you.

## 4. "Everything I Ever Copied" — *synth-pop*
> Neon nights and a history bar,
> every little thing I copied, near and far —
> tag it, search it, watch it glow,
> the clips remember even when I don't. ✨

## 5. "Shanty of the Clipboard Hoard" — *sea shanty*
> Oh the captain copied a treasure map (*heave!*),
> and Cliphoard kept it under the flap (*ho!*),
> encrypted in the hold where the secrets are stowed —
> so paste, me hearties, paste the hoard! 🏴‍☠️

## 6. "Little Clip, Sleep Tight" — *lullaby (encryption-at-rest)*
> Hush now little clip, sealed up tight,
> AES-and-Enclave through the night.
> No one reads you, no one peeks,
> safe on this Mac for a thousand weeks. 🌙

## 7. "No Cloud, No Master" — *punk*
> NO account! NO sync! NO server in the sky!
> Your clipboard's YOURS — we never ask you why!
> Grep the source, we got nothing to hide —
> CLIPHOARD! On-device! On your side! ⚡

## 8. "Ctrl-Opt-Cmd-V" — *15-second jingle / earworm*
> Con-trol — op-tion — com-mand — V! (*ding!*)
> The clip you lost? It's right here, see?
> Cliphoard! (Cliphoard!) Hoard it back —
> the only clipboard with your whole life's stack. 🎵

## 9. "Drop the Clip" — *EDM / festival*
> (build) copy it… stack it… search it… find it…
> wait for it… wait for it…
> **DROP** — *paste!* 💥
> (the bass is the ⌘V landing in your doc)

## 10. "On Your Mac, Forever" — *folk / indie*
> It's a quiet little bag on the edge of the screen,
> the smallest good habit you've ever seen.
> Copy without fear, let it all amass —
> a hoard of your days, kept safe on your Mac. 🌿
